"Masala dosa or masale dose is a variation of the popular South Indian food dosa, which has its origins in Tuluva Mangalorean cuisine.It is made from rice, lentils, potato, methi, and curry leaves, and served with chutneys and sambar. It is popular in South India, it can be found in all other parts of the country and overseas.In South India, preparation of masala dosa varies from city to city." (Source:Wikipedia)
